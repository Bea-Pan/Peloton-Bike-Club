About Kids Bike Clinic Volunteer

Meet Up. Explore. Exercise.

Join our Peloton Bike Club and meet people with similar hobbies & interests. Explore nearby city & country trails in a peloton. Develop healthy living habits and stay fit!

Weekly Rides

We organise free weekly 10km group rides through the city (City Trails - 1st and 3rd Saturday of the month) & in the outskirts (Country Rides - 2nd and 3rd Saturday of the month).

Come and join the fun!

City Trail Saturdays - we meet at the south entrance of the Jubilee Park (behind the ice-cream kiosk) at 9am.


Country Rides Saturdays - we meet by Pomeranian Bridge (east side) at 10am.

"I have recently moved to town and joining Peloton Bike Club has been a great way to make like-minded friends! I especially enjoy Country Rides Saturdays as I like being in the nature. I also enjoyed sharing the cycling passion with the younger generation as volunteer at Kids Sunday Club!"

Celia Corti - Graphic Designer/ Frequent Club Cyclist
