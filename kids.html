About Kids Bike Clinic Volunteer

Kids Sunday Club

Every Sunday we invite kids to take part in a free 5km bike ride followed by one hour of outdoor games and fun. Bring your kids and teach them importance of healthy living & exercise!

Weekly Rides

We organise free weekly 5km group kids rides in the Jubilee Park (Jubilee Park Sundays - all Sundays except the 2nd Sunday of the month) & and 5km rides in the outskirts (Family Expedition Sundays - 2nd Sunday of the month).

Kids Sunday Club locations

Jubilee Park Sundays - our meeting point is on the meadow (near south entrance and tennis courts) at 9am.


Family Expedition Sundays* - we meet by Pomeranian Bridge (east side) at 9am.

*Children must be accompanied by an adult.
